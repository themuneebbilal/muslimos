✦ Everything You Need
One app, complete deen
No more switching between five different apps. MuslimOS brings together every tool a practicing Muslim needs — designed with the beauty and reverence Islam deserves.

Full Quran

All 114 surahs with Arabic text, English and Urdu translations. Per-ayah audio with multiple reciters. Beautiful reading experience.

Prayer Times

Precise calculation using solar position math. Live countdown to next prayer. Multiple calculation methods. Works offline.

Hadith Library

8 major collections including Bukhari, Muslim, and 40 Nawawi. Search, bookmark, and share. Offline access.

Dhikr & Duas

Tasbeeh counter with multiple modes. Morning and evening adhkar with tap-to-count. 35+ duas organized by life context.

Qibla Compass

Real-time direction to the Ka'bah using GPS and device compass. Clean, accurate, and beautifully designed.

Quran Audio

Listen to any surah or individual ayah. Multiple world-class reciters. Persistent player that continues across screens.
