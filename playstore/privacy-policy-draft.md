# MuslimOS Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: March 15, 2026

## Overview

MuslimOS is designed to keep core personal usage data on-device wherever possible.

## Data the app may access

- location
  Used only for prayer times and qibla direction features
- audio playback state
  Used for Quran recitation playback
- local settings and saved content
  Used for bookmarks, favorites, journal entries, reading position, and preferences

## Data storage approach

- bookmarks, favorites, settings, journal content, and reading position are stored locally on the device
- MuslimOS does not currently require account creation
- MuslimOS does not currently sell personal data

## Network usage

MuslimOS may contact third-party services for:

- Quran recitation audio
- tafseer content
- hadith or related API-backed content where applicable

## Permissions

- location permission is optional and used only for prayer times and qibla features
- notification permission may be requested in future versions for prayer reminders

## User control

Users can:

- deny location access
- clear app storage from device settings
- remove saved bookmarks and favorites within the app
