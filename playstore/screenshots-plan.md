# Screenshot Plan

Use this sequence for Play Store screenshots.

## Device targets

- 6.1" Android phone
- 6.7" Android phone

## Capture order

1. Home
   Prayer summary, ayat of the day, and quick access visible
2. Prayer Times
   Active window hero plus remaining prayer cards
3. Quran List
   Surahs and Favorites visible
4. Quran Reader
   Arabic text, translation, and synced playback highlight
5. Worship
   Tasbeeh or adhkar surface
6. Hadith
   Collection overview or reading view
7. Qibla
   Compass screen with direction visible
8. Journal
   Private reflection UI

## Capture rules

- remove browser chrome
- use consistent time and battery if possible
- avoid placeholder states
- avoid permission prompts in screenshots
- prefer real content over empty states
- keep bottom nav visible unless the screen genuinely hides it
