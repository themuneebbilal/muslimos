### Full Description (max 4000 chars)
```
MuslimOS brings your daily Islamic essentials into one focused experience.

Use MuslimOS to:

• Check prayer times with accurate calculations for your location
• Read the full Quran with Arabic text, translations, and tafseer
• Listen to recitation with synced word-by-word highlighting
• Save favorite surahs and bookmark ayahs
• Explore hadith collections from trusted sources
• Use tasbeeh counter and dhikr tools for daily worship
• Track your daily prayers, Quran reading, and dhikr habits
• Find qibla direction with a built-in compass
• Keep a private daily journal with mood tracking
• Follow the Islamic calendar with important dates
• Learn step-by-step guides for salah, wudu, and more

Built with a calm emerald and gold design language, MuslimOS is designed to feel intentional, readable, and respectful across both phone and larger screens.

All data stays on your device. No account required. No ads.
```
